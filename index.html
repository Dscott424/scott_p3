THINK INSIDE THE BOX

Page Title

  • PARTICIPANTS WILL SELECT SHAPES AND
  • COLORS TO CONVEY AN EMOTION OR FEELING.

    • ROUND ONE

    • USERS WILL SELECT A FEELING OR EMOTION TO DEPICT IN THE ART BOX.
    • USERS WILL SELECT THREE COLORS FOR A PALETTE FOR THE FIRST ROUND. (1 BACKGROUND COLOR, 2 FILL COLORS)
    • USERS WILL ALSO SELECT UP TO THREE SHAPES FOR THE FIRST ROUND.
    • PRESS "S" TO SAVE IMAGE

    ROUND TWO

    • IF YOU CHOSE ONE EMOTION OR FEELING, THEN YOU WILL SELECT THE OPPOSITE FEELING OR EMOTION.
    • USE THE SAME SHAPES YOU USED IN THE FIRST ROUND.
    • USERS WILL SELECT A NEW THREE COLORS FOR A PALETTE FOR THE SECOND ROUND. (1 BACKGROUND COLOR, 2 FILL COLORS)
    • PRESS "S" TO SAVE IMAGE.

    ROUND THREE

    • USERS WILL SELECT A NEW FEELING OR EMOTION TO DEPICT IN THE ART BOX.
    • USERS WILL USE ALL THE COLORS USED FROM THE PREVIOUS TWO ROUNDS.(1 BACKGROUND COLOR, 5 FILL COLORS USING 1 OF THE BACKGROUND COLORS)
    • PRESS "S" TO SAVE IMAGE.
